About WAU

👁️

Our Vision

A thriving, united African community in Wenzhou where every member has the opportunity to succeed, connect, and celebrate our rich cultural heritage while building bridges with our Chinese hosts.

🌍

Our Mission

The Wenzhou African Union exists to unite, empower, and celebrate the African community in Wenzhou, China. We foster cultural exchange, support African businesses, and create a home away from home for every African residing in this vibrant city.

📅

About Africa Day

Africa Day, celebrated on May 25th each year, commemorates the founding of the Organisation of African Unity (now the African Union) in 1963. It's a day to celebrate the progress, culture, and unity of the African continent.

Our 2026 celebration in Wenzhou brings together all 54 African nations' representatives for a day of recognition, celebration, and community building.
