About WAU

👁️

Our Vision

A thriving, united African community in Wenzhou where every member has the opportunity to succeed, connect, and celebrate our rich cultural heritage while building bridges with our Chinese hosts.

🌍

Our Mission

The Wenzhou African Union exists to unite, empower, and celebrate the African community in Wenzhou, China. We foster cultural exchange, support African businesses, and create a home away from home.

📅

About Africa Day

Africa Day, celebrated on May 25th each year, commemorates the founding of the Organisation of African Unity (now the African Union) in 1963.

Our 2026 celebration brings together all 54 African nations' representatives for recognition, celebration, and community building.
